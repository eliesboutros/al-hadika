Live Website

https://al-hadika.com

About

This project is the official website for Al-Hadika.

The site provides information about the company’s pest control and garden services and allows customers to contact the business directly online. It also integrates tools like WhatsApp and Google Maps to make communication and location access easier.

Main goals of the website:

Clearly present the services offered

Allow customers to contact the business through a simple form

Show business hours with a live open / closed status

Provide quick contact through WhatsApp

Display the company location on Google Maps
